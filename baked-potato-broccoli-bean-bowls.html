Baked Potato Broccoli Bean Bowls

A filling bowl meal built from baked potatoes, broccoli, beans, and a simple savory topping.

Baked Potato Broccoli Bean Bowls

Prep time: 15 minutes - Cook time: 35 minutes - Total time: 50 minutes
Estimated total cost: $12.80 - Estimated cost per serving: $3.20 - Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes — $3.00
  • 1 large broccoli crown or 2 cups frozen broccoli — $1.80
  • 1 can white beans or chickpeas, drained and rinsed — $1.00
  • 1 tablespoon oil — $0.20
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder — $0.10
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika — $0.05
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt — $0.05
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper — $0.05
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast or simple sauce, optional — $0.60
  • Optional: hot sauce or lemon juice — $0.20

Estimated ingredient total: $7.05 to $7.65
Displayed planning estimate: $12.80

Detailed Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
  2. Scrub the potatoes, pierce them with a fork, and place them on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 35 to 45 minutes until tender.
  4. While the potatoes bake, chop the broccoli if using fresh.
  5. Steam or sauté the broccoli until tender-crisp.
  6. Warm the beans in a small pan with garlic powder, paprika, salt, and black pepper.
  7. Split the baked potatoes open once they are done.
  8. Fluff the inside lightly with a fork.
  9. Top each potato with broccoli and seasoned beans.
  10. Sprinkle with nutritional yeast or drizzle with a simple sauce if using.
  11. Finish with hot sauce or lemon juice if desired and serve hot.

Helpful Notes

  • Sweet potatoes also work here.
  • This is a very flexible meal for leftovers.
  • Frozen broccoli keeps prep easy and cheap.
