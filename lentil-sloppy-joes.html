Lentil Sloppy Joes

A budget-friendly lentil filling that turns simple sandwich buns into a filling meal.

Lentil Sloppy Joes

Prep time: 10 minutes • Cook time: 25 minutes • Total time: 35 minutes
Estimated total cost: $8.20 • Estimated cost per serving: $2.05 • Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Cooked lentils
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce or ketchup
  • Garlic
  • Mustard or seasoning
  • Sandwich buns

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add cooked lentils, tomato sauce, and seasonings.
  3. Simmer until thickened into a loose sandwich filling.
  4. Spoon onto buns and serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • Serve with slaw or roasted potatoes if you want a fuller meal.
  • The filling works well for leftovers.
