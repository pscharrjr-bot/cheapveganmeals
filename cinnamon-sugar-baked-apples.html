Cinnamon Sugar Baked Apples

A warm, simple dessert made from baked apples, cinnamon, and a little sugar for a low-cost cozy treat.

Cinnamon Sugar Baked Apples

Prep time: 10 minutes - Cook time: 25 minutes - Total time: 35 minutes
Estimated total cost: $5.80 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.45 - Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 apples, sliced — $3.20
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar — $0.15
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon — $0.10
  • 1 tablespoon oil or vegan butter — $0.20
  • Optional: oats or chopped nuts for topping — $0.50

Detailed Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F.
  2. Wash, core, and slice the apples.
  3. Place the apple slices in a baking dish.
  4. Drizzle with oil or dot with vegan butter.
  5. Sprinkle the brown sugar and cinnamon over the apples.
  6. Toss lightly so the apples are coated evenly.
  7. Add oats or nuts on top if using.
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes until the apples are soft and slightly caramelized.
  9. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
  10. Serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • This is great on its own or over oatmeal.
  • Firm apples hold their shape best.
  • A pinch of salt can make the flavor pop.
