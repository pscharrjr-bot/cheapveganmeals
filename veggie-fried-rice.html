Veggie Fried Rice

A quick, flexible, low-cost meal that makes especially good use of leftover rice and frozen vegetables.

Veggie Fried Rice

Prep time: 10 minutes - Cook time: 12 minutes - Total time: 22 minutes
Estimated total cost: $6.80 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.70 - Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Frozen mixed vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Optional tofu or edamame

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet.
  2. Add vegetables and cook until hot.
  3. Add garlic and then stir in the rice.
  4. Season with soy sauce and cook until everything is evenly heated.

Helpful Notes

  • Day-old rice works best for texture.
  • Add extra soy sauce or chili crisp to taste.
