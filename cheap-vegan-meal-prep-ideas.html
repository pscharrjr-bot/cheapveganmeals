Cheap Vegan Meal Prep Ideas

Meal prep can reduce cost, stress, and last-minute takeout decisions when the week gets busy.

Good cheap prep formats

  • Lentil chili
  • Rice bowls
  • Pasta salad
  • Overnight oats
  • Burrito bowls

Why prep helps

Prep does not need to mean making seven identical containers. Even partial prep like cooked grains, chopped vegetables, or one large soup pot can make the week cheaper and easier.
