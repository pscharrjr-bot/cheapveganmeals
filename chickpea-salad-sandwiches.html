Chickpea Salad Sandwiches

An easy lunch recipe that turns basic chickpeas into a satisfying sandwich filling.

Chickpea Salad Sandwiches

Prep time: 12 minutes - Cook time: 0 minutes - Total time: 12 minutes
Estimated total cost: $7.40 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.85 - Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Vegan mayo or mashed avocado
  • Mustard
  • Celery or onion
  • Bread or buns

Instructions

  1. Mash the chickpeas lightly in a bowl.
  2. Mix in mayo, mustard, and chopped crunchy vegetables.
  3. Taste and season.
  4. Serve on bread or buns.

Helpful Notes

  • This works well for packed lunches.
  • Serve with lettuce or pickles if available.
