Rice Pudding with Cinnamon

A simple stovetop dessert made from rice, plant milk, cinnamon, and a little sweetener.

Rice Pudding with Cinnamon

Prep time: 5 minutes - Cook time: 25 minutes - Total time: 30 minutes
Estimated total cost: $5.20 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.30 - Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice — $0.40
  • 2 cups plant milk — $1.20
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or maple syrup — $0.15
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon — $0.05
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, optional — $0.10
  • Pinch of salt — $0.02
  • Optional: raisins or banana slices — $0.50

Detailed Instructions

  1. Add the cooked rice, plant milk, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla if using, and salt to a small saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium-low heat.
  3. Cook for 20 to 25 minutes, stirring often, until the mixture thickens and becomes creamy.
  4. If it gets too thick, add a small splash of plant milk.
  5. Taste and adjust sweetness.
  6. Stir in raisins if using.
  7. Spoon into bowls.
  8. Serve warm or chilled.

Helpful Notes

  • Leftover rice works perfectly here.
  • This can be eaten warm in colder weather or chilled in warmer months.
  • A little extra cinnamon on top makes it look nicer.
