Lentil Chili

A cheap, hearty, batch-friendly chili that works especially well for leftovers.

Lentil Chili

Estimated total cost: $13.80 • Estimated cost per serving: $2.30 • Serves 6

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Dry lentils
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Beans
  • Onion
  • Chili seasoning

Instructions

Cook onion and seasonings, add lentils, tomatoes, beans, and water or broth, then simmer until the lentils are tender and the chili thickens.
