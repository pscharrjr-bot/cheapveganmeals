Banana Nice Cream

Estimated total cost: $3.80 • Estimated cost per serving: $1.27 • Serves 3

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Frozen bananas
  • Plant milk splash
  • Cocoa or peanut butter optional

Instructions

Blend frozen bananas until creamy, add a small splash of plant milk if needed, then serve immediately or freeze briefly for a firmer texture.
