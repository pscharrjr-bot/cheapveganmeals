Banana Nice Cream

A cheap frozen dessert made mostly from bananas, with optional add-ins for extra flavor.

Banana Nice Cream

Prep time: 10 minutes • Cook time: 0 minutes • Total time: 10 minutes, plus freezing time
Estimated total cost: $3.80 • Estimated cost per serving: $1.27 • Serves: 3

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas, sliced and frozen — $1.40
  • 2 to 4 tablespoons plant milk — $0.20
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter or cocoa powder, optional — $0.30
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, optional — $0.10
  • Optional topping: chopped peanuts or chocolate drizzle — $0.40

Detailed Instructions

  1. Slice the bananas ahead of time and freeze them until solid, ideally for several hours.
  2. Add the frozen banana slices to a food processor or high-powered blender.
  3. Blend, stopping to scrape down the sides as needed.
  4. At first the bananas will look crumbly, then gradually become smooth and creamy.
  5. Add a small splash of plant milk only if the blender needs help moving.
  6. Blend in peanut butter, cocoa powder, or vanilla if using.
  7. Serve immediately for a soft-serve texture.
  8. For a firmer texture, transfer to a container and freeze for 20 to 30 minutes before scooping.
  9. Add toppings just before serving.
