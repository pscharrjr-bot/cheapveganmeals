Potato Chickpea Curry

Cheap, comforting, and filling, with potatoes and chickpeas doing most of the heavy lifting.

Potato Chickpea Curry

Prep time: 10 minutes • Cook time: 25 minutes • Total time: 35 minutes
Estimated total cost: $8.90 • Estimated cost per serving: $2.23 • Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Curry powder
  • Canned tomatoes or water
  • Rice for serving

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic with curry powder.
  2. Add diced potatoes, chickpeas, and liquid.
  3. Simmer until the potatoes are tender.
  4. Serve over rice.

Helpful Notes

  • Use more water for a thinner curry.
  • Frozen peas work well if you want extra vegetables.
