Black Bean Taco Rice Skillet

A one-pan dinner that keeps cost low by using rice, black beans, and familiar taco-style seasoning.

Black Bean Taco Rice Skillet

Prep time: 10 minutes • Cook time: 20 minutes • Total time: 30 minutes
Estimated total cost: $7.80 • Estimated cost per serving: $1.95 • Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked rice
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 cup salsa or canned tomatoes
  • Taco seasoning or cumin/chili powder/garlic

Instructions

  1. Sauté the onion and pepper until softened.
  2. Add the rice, beans, salsa, seasonings, and enough water to cook the rice.
  3. Simmer covered until the rice is tender and most of the liquid is absorbed.
  4. Fluff, taste, and serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • Add corn if you already have it.
  • This is easy to portion for leftovers.
  • Hot sauce works well on top.
