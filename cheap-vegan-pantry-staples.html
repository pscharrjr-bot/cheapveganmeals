Cheap Vegan Pantry Staples

Keeping a few flexible budget ingredients on hand makes cheap vegan cooking much more realistic.

Staples worth keeping around

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Beans
  • Oats
  • Pasta
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Peanut butter
  • Frozen vegetables

Why pantry staples matter

When your base ingredients are already in the house, it becomes easier to build meals without expensive last-minute grocery trips. Pantry stability lowers cost and reduces the chance of defaulting to takeout.
