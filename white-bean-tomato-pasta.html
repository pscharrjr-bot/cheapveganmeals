White Bean Tomato Pasta

A cheap pantry dinner that stretches pasta with tomatoes and white beans for more staying power.

White Bean Tomato Pasta

Prep time: 10 minutes - Cook time: 20 minutes - Total time: 30 minutes
Estimated total cost: $8.60 - Estimated cost per serving: $2.15 - Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • White beans
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in salted water.
  2. Sauté garlic in oil, then add tomatoes and seasoning.
  3. Stir in beans and simmer briefly.
  4. Toss the sauce with the cooked pasta and serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • Use any bean you already have if white beans are unavailable.
  • This is easy to bulk up with spinach.
