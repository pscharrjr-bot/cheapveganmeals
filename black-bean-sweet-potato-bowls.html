Black Bean Sweet Potato Bowls

A filling, colorful bowl meal built from simple ingredients that stretch well across multiple servings.

Black Bean Sweet Potato Bowls

Prep time: 15 minutes • Cook time: 25 minutes • Total time: 40 minutes
Estimated total cost: $9.30 • Estimated cost per serving: $2.33 • Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Sweet potatoes
  • Black beans
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Spices or taco seasoning

Instructions

  1. Roast or sauté the sweet potatoes until tender.
  2. Cook the rice.
  3. Warm the black beans with seasoning.
  4. Build bowls with rice, sweet potato, and beans.

Helpful Notes

  • Add salsa or lime if you have it.
  • This is a great meal-prep option.
