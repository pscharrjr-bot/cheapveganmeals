Black Bean Sweet Potato Bowls

A cheap, filling bowl meal built from sweet potatoes, black beans, and rice with warm seasoning.

Black Bean Sweet Potato Bowls

Prep time: 15 minutes - Cook time: 25 minutes - Total time: 40 minutes
Estimated total cost: $9.30 - Estimated cost per serving: $2.33 - Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced - $2.00
  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed - $1.00
  • 2 cups cooked rice - $0.80
  • 1 tablespoon oil - $0.20
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder - $0.15
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin - $0.05
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt - $0.05
  • 1 small onion, diced - $0.80
  • Optional: salsa, lime juice, or cilantro - $0.70

Detailed Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F if roasting the sweet potatoes, or use a skillet if you prefer stovetop cooking.
  2. Peel and dice the sweet potatoes into small cubes.
  3. Toss the sweet potatoes with half the oil, chili powder, cumin, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Spread them on a baking sheet and roast for 20 to 25 minutes, flipping once, until tender and lightly browned.
  5. While the sweet potatoes cook, heat the remaining oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Add the onion and cook for 4 to 5 minutes until softened.
  7. Add the black beans and cook until warmed through.
  8. Reheat the rice if needed.
  9. Divide the rice among 4 bowls.
  10. Top with the roasted sweet potatoes and black beans.
  11. Add optional salsa, lime juice, or cilantro.
  12. Serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • You can pan-cook the sweet potatoes if you do not want to use the oven.
  • This recipe works well for meal prep.
  • Add shredded cabbage for extra crunch if you have it.
