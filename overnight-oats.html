Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract, optional - $0.10

Detailed Instructions

  1. Add the oats, plant milk, water, chia seeds if using, cinnamon, sweetener, and vanilla to a large bowl or storage container.
  2. Stir well so the oats are fully coated.
  3. Slice one banana and stir it in, or save all fruit for topping later.
  4. Cover the container and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 6 hours.
  5. The next morning, stir the oats well.
  6. If the mixture seems too thick, add a splash of plant milk.
  7. Divide into portions and top with the remaining fruit.
  8. Serve cold, or warm briefly in the microwave if preferred.

Helpful Notes

  • Mason jars work well for individual portions.
  • Chia seeds make the oats thicker and more pudding-like.
  • Peanut butter is an easy extra topping if you want more staying power.
