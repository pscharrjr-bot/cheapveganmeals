Detailed Instructions

  1. Add the oats, water or plant milk, cinnamon, and salt to a medium saucepan.
  2. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer over medium heat.
  3. Stir in one of the bananas, either sliced or mashed, so it softens into the oats.
  4. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring often, until the oats are thick and creamy.
  5. Stir in the peanut butter and sweetener until fully combined.
  6. Taste and add a little more cinnamon or sweetener if desired.
  7. Divide into 4 bowls.
  8. Top with the remaining banana and any optional toppings.
  9. Serve warm.
