Peanut Butter Banana Toast Plate

A very cheap breakfast or snack-style meal built from staples many people already keep around.

Peanut Butter Banana Toast Plate

Prep time: 5 minutes - Cook time: 5 minutes - Total time: 10 minutes
Estimated total cost: $5.60 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.40 - Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Bananas
  • Cinnamon optional

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread lightly if desired.
  2. Spread peanut butter on each slice.
  3. Top with banana slices and a little cinnamon.
  4. Serve immediately.

Helpful Notes

  • This works for breakfast or a quick afternoon meal.
  • Use sunflower seed butter if needed instead.
