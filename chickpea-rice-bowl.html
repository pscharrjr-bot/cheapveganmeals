Chickpea Rice Bowl

A simple, filling, budget-friendly vegan bowl built from pantry staples.

Chickpea Rice Bowl

Estimated total cost: $8.40 • Estimated cost per serving: $2.10 • Serves 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 cans chickpeas
  • 1 onion
  • 1 bell pepper
  • Garlic, paprika, cumin, salt

Instructions

Sauté onion and pepper, add chickpeas and spices, warm through, then serve over rice. Adjust seasoning and add any simple toppings you like.
