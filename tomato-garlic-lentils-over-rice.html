Tomato Garlic Lentils Over Rice

A cheap, comforting lentil dinner that leans on pantry staples and simple seasoning.

Tomato Garlic Lentils Over Rice

Prep time: 10 minutes • Cook time: 30 minutes • Total time: 40 minutes
Estimated total cost: $7.70 • Estimated cost per serving: $1.93 • Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Rice
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oil and seasonings

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice separately.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in oil.
  3. Add lentils, tomatoes, water, and seasonings.
  4. Simmer until lentils are soft and spoon over rice.

Helpful Notes

  • This keeps well for leftovers.
  • Add spinach if you want more greens.
