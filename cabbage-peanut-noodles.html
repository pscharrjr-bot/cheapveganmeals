Cabbage Peanut Noodles

A lower-cost noodle variation that stretches a simple peanut sauce with sautéed cabbage.

Cabbage Peanut Noodles

Prep time: 10 minutes - Cook time: 15 minutes - Total time: 25 minutes
Estimated total cost: $6.90 - Estimated cost per serving: $1.73 - Serves: 4

Recipe image placeholder

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces noodles
  • 1/2 small cabbage, sliced
  • 1/3 to 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Warm water

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles and drain them.
  2. Sauté the cabbage until softened but not mushy.
  3. Mix peanut butter, soy sauce, garlic, and warm water into a smooth sauce.
  4. Toss noodles and cabbage with the sauce and serve warm.

Helpful Notes

  • Cabbage is a strong budget stretcher.
  • This also works cold as leftovers.
